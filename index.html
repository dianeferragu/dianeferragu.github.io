Research

My research examines **how microplastics accumulate in human tissues and their long-term biological impact**. Using **Random Forest and XGBoost models**, I analyze correlations between **chemical class, age at exposure, and retention levels** to determine their influence on microplastic toxicity.

Findings suggest that **younger individuals and endocrine system exposure pose higher risks**, emphasizing the need for better monitoring and regulation of plastic exposure in everyday environments.
