Diane Ferragu

I am a researcher in bioengineering and computational biology, focusing on machine learning applications for biological systems. My recent work explores the **accumulation of microplastics in human biological systems** and their toxicological and physiological effects.

I am particularly interested in how environmental contaminants interact with human health and the role of AI in uncovering these patterns. My research combines **computational modeling and experimental validation** to understand these complex biological interactions.
